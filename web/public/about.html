About Us

This is This Side of Polling, a vanity project of Merlin Heidemanns. I came to election forecasting by accident with my advisor at the time asking me whether I wanted to participate in helping The Economist build their forecast for the U.S. Presidential election in 2020. One thing led to another and by way of an unimaginative spirit and desire to finish I continued working on forecasting for my dissertation project.
This project here was borne out of my continued interest in the area. Polling and election forecasting interest me because it has a practical relationship to politics circumscribing peoples' vote intentions (rather than say stockmarket valuations), you can be wrong given an observed election outcome but also future polls, and lastly because there are so many factors floating around that make the task non-trivial. And I enjoy coding in Stan.
